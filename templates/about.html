About

Mutalyzer is designed and developed by Jeroen F. J. Laros, with the following exceptions:
  • The LRG parser, as well as the Batch interfaces are written by Gerben R. Stouten.
  • The position converter interfaces (webservice and WWW) are written by Gerard C. P. Schaafsma.
Specifications are given by Peter E. M. Taschner and Johan T. den Dunnen.

Since the publication is under development, please use the old reference for now when referring to these pages: "Wildeman M et al. (2008). Improving sequence variant descriptions in mutation databases and literature using the Mutalyzer sequence variation nomenclature checker. Hum Mutat 29, 6-13".
