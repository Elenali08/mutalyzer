Beste Peter,

dit weekeinde heb ik Mutalyzer 2.0 gebruikt in LOVD, een geweldige 
verbetering, mijn complimenten, veel sneelr en duidelijker. Toch enkele 
kleine opmerkingen;

* ik zou een regel over RNA toevoegen; assuming you have not analysed 
RNA, the predicted effect on RNA level can best be described as r.(?)
NOTE r.(?) indicates RNA was not analysed but there is no reason to 
suggest that the consequences are other then those on DNA level.

Voor varianten op posities +1, +2, -2, -1;
assuming you have not analysed RNA, the predicted effect on RNA level 
can best be described as r.spl?
NOTE r.spl? indicates RNA was not analysed but that it is highly likely 
that the change directly affects RNA splicing

Voor varianten op posities +3 to +6 en -30 to -3
assuming you have not analysed RNA, the predicted effect on RNA level 
can best be described as r.(spl?)
NOTE r.(spl?) indicates RNA was not analysed but that it can not be 
excluded that the change affects RNA splicing

Voor varianten op posities +7 to -31
assuming you have not analysed RNA, the predicted effect on RNA level 
can best be described as r.(=)
NOTE r.(=) indicates RNA was not analysed but that it is not likely that 
the change affects RNA splicing


Idem, voor alle varianten op protein level deze tussen haakjes geven met 
de opmerking;
assuming you have not analysed RNA, the predicted effect on protein 
level is given between brackets to indicate that it is a prediction only.

Met vriendelijke groet,
